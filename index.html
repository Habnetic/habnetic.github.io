About

Habnetic is an open research initiative exploring probabilistic models of housing resilience and adaptive habitat design. Developed independently by Mikel Martínez, it bridges architecture, data science, and Bayesian reasoning to understand how built environments respond under uncertainty.

Research

The project combines data from open scientific sources such as NASA, ESA, and UN Habitat to model how housing systems behave under stochastic events. Its long-term goal is to extend these frameworks from terrestrial housing to extraplanetary habitats.

All models and datasets are released under open-source licenses to encourage transparency and collaboration.
